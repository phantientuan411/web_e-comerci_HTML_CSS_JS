Our Story

We are a team of four second-year students — Hien, Tuan, Quyet, Tho — currently majoring in Software Engineering at Electric Power University. Driven by our passion for technology and a strong desire to turn knowledge into real-world products, we created this electronic e-commerce website as our very first milestone.

This project is more than just an academic assignment; it represents our journey of applying what we have learned in programming, web development, and system design into a fully functional platform. Every line of code and every interface reflects our dedication, teamwork, and continuous effort to improve.

Our goal is to provide customers with high-quality electronic products at reasonable prices, along with a convenient and reliable shopping experience. At the same time, this project allows us to sharpen our skills, gain practical experience, and prepare ourselves for future careers as software engineers.

We understand that this is only the beginning. With passion, curiosity, and a commitment to learning, we believe each step we take today will build a strong foundation for our future in the world of technology.
